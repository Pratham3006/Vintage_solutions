Our Services

Comprehensive Packaging Solutions

We offer a wide spectrum of services to provide customized packaging solutions based on client requirements. We cater to industries such as oil and gas, heavy engineering, pharmaceuticals, FMCG manufacturers, bulk drugs, and chemical industries.

ISPM-15 Container Fumigation

Container fumigation is a specialized treatment process aimed at eliminating or preventing the presence of pests and insects within shipping containers. This meticulous procedure involves the application of fumigants to eradicate any potential infestations or contaminants, ensuring that the container and its contents meet international phytosanitary standards.

  • ISPM-15 Heat Treatment
  • Methyl Bromide Fumigation
  • International Compliance Certified

Lashing & Chocking

Industrial lashing and chocking are essential cargo securing techniques. Lashing involves using straps or chains to prevent cargo movement, ensuring stability during transport. Chocking uses physical restraints to secure individual items. These practices enhance safety, minimize shifting, and maintain cargo integrity during transit.

  • Heavy-duty cargo securing
  • Container lashing services
  • Professional chocking solutions

Dunnage (Air Bag) Packing

Dunnage bag packing ensures cargo stability, preventing damage during shipment. Designed to prevent movement and protect goods during transit, these air-filled bags serve as effective cushions, minimizing the risk of damage caused by shifting or collisions. This method enhances cargo stability, optimizes loading efficiency, and ensures the safe and secure transport of goods.

Pallet Packing & Palletization

Pallet packing is a practical and efficient approach to secure goods during shipping. By organizing and securely fastening items onto pallets, this method simplifies handling, enhances stability, and facilitates seamless loading and unloading. It optimizes storage space, streamlines logistics, and ensures the safe and organized transport of goods.

  • Palletizing
  • Crating
  • Heavy packing in boxes

Pallet Chemical Treatment

Professional chemical treatment services for wooden pallets to meet international export standards and prevent pest infestation during international shipping.

On-Site Packing Services

We provide packaging services both at warehouse locations and directly at factory premises, enabling our clients to expand their business activities efficiently while maintaining high-quality packaging standards. Our mobile teams are equipped to handle all packing requirements at your location.
